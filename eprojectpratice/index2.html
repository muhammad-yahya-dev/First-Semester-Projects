Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions.

🎯

How accurate are conversion results?

+

Our tools use standard international definitions for conversions (e.g., 1 inch = 2.54 cm exactly). For currency, we use live market rates updated hourly. Scientific conversions are precise up to decimal places displayed.

📶

Do I need an internet connection to use website?

+

No! Once the page is loaded, all metric and standard unit conversions happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. You do not need an active internet connection for calculations, only for fetching live currency rates initially.

⚓

What is a knot? What is a nautical mile?

+

A knot is one nautical mile per hour. One nautical mile is defined as exactly 1,852 meters. It is primarily used for air and marine navigation.

💰

How often are currency exchange rates updated?

+

Our currency exchange rates are sourced from major financial markets and are updated automatically every hour to ensure you get the most current conversion possible.

📱

Can I use this website on my mobile phone?

+

Yes! The website is fully responsive and optimized for smartphones and tablets. We also offer dedicated native apps for both iOS and Android for a smoother offline experience.

🔐

Is my data private?

+

Absolutely. All conversions are performed locally in your browser. We do not store, track, or transmit the numbers you input to any external servers. Your privacy is our priority.

🌡️

How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit manually?

+

The formula is: (°C × 9/5) + 32 = °F. To convert from Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 and multiply by 5/9: (°F - 32) × 5/9 = °C.

⚖️

Is the mobile app free?

+

Yes! Our mobile applications for both iOS and Android are free to download and use. We offer a premium ad-free version for users who want to support development.

🖨️

Can I print conversion tables?

+

Yes. You can use your browser's "Print" function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to print any of our conversion tables or charts directly to PDF or paper for offline reference.

🛠️

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

+

We love feedback! You can contact our support team directly through the "Contact Support" link in the footer, or message us on our social media channels found below.
